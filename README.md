Sales Dashboard

1. Project Title
Global Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI | KPI & Profitability Analysis

2. Short Description
This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of global sales performance, customer trends, and product profitability. It is designed to help business stakeholders monitor key metrics, identify high-performing regions and customers, and detect loss-making products for better decision-making.

3. Tech Stack
Power BI
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Power Query (ETL & Data Transformation)
Excel
SQL

4. Data Source
Sample Superstore Dataset (Excel)
Data includes sales, customer, product, and regional information

5. Features
KPI Tracking: Sales ($13M), Quantity (178K), Avg Delivery Time, Return Orders
Regional Analysis: Sales distribution across global markets
Segment Insights: Revenue breakdown by customer segments
Customer Analysis: Top 10 customers contributing to revenue
Profitability Insights:
Top 5 profit-generating products
Top 5 loss-making products
Map Visualization: Geographic representation of sales performance
Interactive Filters: Year-wise analysis (2012–2015)

6. Key Insights
Consumer segment contributes the highest share of total sales
Asia Pacific region leads in overall revenue generation
Certain products are consistently generating losses and require attention
A small group of top customers drives a significant portion of revenue
